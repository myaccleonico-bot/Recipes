Feijoada

Feijoada is a black bean stew cooked with pork and sausage until deep and rich.

Ingredients

What You Need

  • 2 cups cooked black beans
  • 200 g pork chunks
  • 150 g smoked sausage, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups water or stock
  • 1 bay leaf, salt, and pepper
Instructions

How to Cook It

  1. Brown the pork and sausage in a heavy pot.
  2. Add onion and garlic, then cook until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the beans, bay leaf, and stock.
  4. Simmer until thick and flavorful.
  5. Serve hot, usually with rice on the side.

Moqueca

Moqueca is a Brazilian seafood stew with coconut milk, peppers, tomatoes, and herbs.

Ingredients

What You Need

  • 500 g firm fish pieces
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Cilantro and lime
Instructions

How to Cook It

  1. Layer onion, bell pepper, tomatoes, and fish in a pot.
  2. Season lightly and drizzle with oil.
  3. Pour in the coconut milk and cover.
  4. Simmer gently until the fish is cooked through.
  5. Finish with cilantro and lime before serving.
Recipe 3

Pao de Queijo

Pao de Queijo is a Brazilian cheese bread made from tapioca flour and baked until puffy.

Ingredients

What You Need

  • 2 cups tapioca flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup grated cheese
  • Salt
Instructions

How to Cook It

  1. Heat the milk and oil together until hot but not boiling hard.
  2. Pour over the tapioca flour and mix carefully.
  3. Let it cool slightly, then mix in the egg, cheese, and salt.
  4. Shape into small balls and place on a tray.
  5. Bake until puffed and lightly golden.
