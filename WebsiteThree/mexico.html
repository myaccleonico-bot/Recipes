Tacos al Pastor

Tacos al Pastor pairs marinated pork with warm tortillas, pineapple, onion, and cilantro.

Ingredients

What You Need

  • 500 g pork, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp achiote paste
  • 1/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Corn tortillas
  • Diced onion, cilantro, and pineapple
Instructions

How to Cook It

  1. Mix achiote paste, pineapple juice, garlic, and cumin, then coat the pork.
  2. Marinate the pork for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Cook the pork in a hot pan until browned and slightly caramelized.
  4. Warm the tortillas in a separate pan.
  5. Fill with pork and top with onion, cilantro, and pineapple.
Recipe 2

Guacamole

Guacamole is a simple avocado dip with lime, onion, tomato, herbs, and optional chili.

Ingredients

What You Need

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 lime
  • 1/4 onion, finely diced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • Cilantro
  • Salt and optional chili
Instructions

How to Make It

  1. Scoop the avocado flesh into a bowl.
  2. Add lime juice and mash to your preferred texture.
  3. Mix in onion, tomato, cilantro, and chili if using.
  4. Season with salt and taste for balance.
  5. Serve right away with chips or tacos.
Recipe 3

Chilaquiles

Chilaquiles uses tortilla chips simmered in salsa and finished with egg, cheese, onion, and cream.

Ingredients

What You Need

  • Tortilla chips
  • 1 cup salsa roja or salsa verde
  • 2 eggs
  • Queso fresco or grated cheese
  • Crema or sour cream
  • Sliced onion
Instructions

How to Cook It

  1. Warm the salsa in a pan until it starts to bubble.
  2. Add the tortilla chips and toss briefly so they soften slightly.
  3. Cook the eggs separately the way you prefer.
  4. Plate the chips and top with eggs, cheese, onion, and crema.
  5. Serve immediately while the texture is still lively.
