Sushi

Sushi uses seasoned rice, nori, and fillings like seafood, cucumber, or avocado for a light and careful presentation.

Ingredients

What You Need

  • 2 cups cooked sushi rice
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 4 nori sheets
  • 1 cucumber, sliced into strips
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • Cooked crab sticks or fresh salmon
  • Soy sauce for serving
Instructions

How to Cook It

  1. Season the cooked rice with rice vinegar and let it cool slightly.
  2. Place a nori sheet on a bamboo mat and spread a thin layer of rice over it.
  3. Add cucumber, avocado, and seafood in the center.
  4. Roll tightly and seal the edge with a little water.
  5. Slice and serve with soy sauce.
Recipe 2

Ramen

Ramen is a warming noodle bowl with broth, soy seasoning, sliced meat, egg, and toppings.

Ingredients

What You Need

  • 200 g ramen noodles
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp miso paste if available
  • Sliced chicken or pork
  • Boiled egg
  • Scallions and mushrooms
Instructions

How to Cook It

  1. Heat the broth and season it with soy sauce and miso.
  2. Cook the ramen noodles separately according to the package.
  3. Warm the sliced meat and prepare the egg and vegetables.
  4. Place the noodles in bowls and pour the hot broth over them.
  5. Top with meat, egg, scallions, and mushrooms before serving.
Recipe 3

Tempura

Tempura is a light fried dish where vegetables or shrimp are coated in a thin cold batter and fried until crisp.

Ingredients

What You Need

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup ice-cold water
  • 1 egg
  • Shrimp or sliced vegetables
  • Oil for frying
  • Salt
Instructions

How to Cook It

  1. Heat the oil in a deep pan while you prepare the batter.
  2. Whisk the egg with ice-cold water, then stir in the flour lightly.
  3. Dip the shrimp or vegetables into the batter.
  4. Fry in small batches until pale golden and crisp.
  5. Drain on paper and serve right away.
