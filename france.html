Coq au Vin

Coq au Vin is chicken slowly cooked with wine, mushrooms, onion, and carrots until deeply flavorful.

Ingredients

What You Need

  • 1 kg chicken pieces
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 150 g mushrooms
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic and 2 tbsp butter or oil
Instructions

How to Cook It

  1. Brown the chicken in butter or oil, then set it aside.
  2. Cook onion, carrots, garlic, and mushrooms in the same pot.
  3. Pour in the wine and stock and stir well.
  4. Return the chicken and simmer gently until tender.
  5. Reduce the sauce slightly more if needed before serving.

Ratatouille

Ratatouille is a vegetable stew made with eggplant, zucchini, tomatoes, peppers, and herbs.

Ingredients

What You Need

  • 1 eggplant, diced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Olive oil and dried herbs
Instructions

How to Cook It

  1. Heat olive oil and cook the onion and garlic first.
  2. Add eggplant and bell pepper, then cook until they soften.
  3. Add zucchini and tomatoes with dried herbs.
  4. Simmer gently until all vegetables are tender.
  5. Serve warm as a side or light main dish.
Recipe 3

Quiche Lorraine

Quiche Lorraine is a baked savory tart with egg, cream, cheese, and bacon in a crust.

Ingredients

What You Need

  • 1 pie crust
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup cream or milk
  • 150 g bacon, cooked
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese
  • Salt and pepper
Instructions

How to Cook It

  1. Fit the crust into a pie dish.
  2. Whisk the eggs with cream, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread bacon and cheese over the crust.
  4. Pour the egg mixture on top.
  5. Bake until the filling is set and lightly golden.
