Carbonara

Carbonara is a Roman pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, black pepper, and pancetta for a silky finish.

Ingredients

What You Need

  • 250 g spaghetti
  • 2 whole eggs and 1 egg yolk
  • 1 cup grated parmesan or pecorino
  • 150 g pancetta or bacon
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • Salt for pasta water
Instructions

How to Cook It

  1. Boil the pasta until al dente and reserve some pasta water.
  2. Cook the pancetta until crisp in a pan.
  3. Whisk the eggs, yolk, cheese, and black pepper together.
  4. Take the pan off the heat and add the hot pasta.
  5. Mix in the egg mixture with a little pasta water until the sauce turns silky.

Margherita Pizza

Margherita Pizza uses simple toppings like tomato sauce, mozzarella, basil, and olive oil on a crisp crust.

Ingredients

What You Need

  • 1 pizza dough ball
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 200 g mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt if needed
Instructions

How to Cook It

  1. Stretch the dough into a round on a lightly floured surface.
  2. Spread tomato sauce thinly over the dough.
  3. Add mozzarella evenly on top.
  4. Bake in a very hot oven until the crust is golden and crisp.
  5. Finish with basil and a drizzle of olive oil before serving.
Recipe 3

Lasagna

Lasagna is a layered baked pasta with meat sauce, cheese, and creamy filling that turns bubbly in the oven.

Ingredients

What You Need

  • Lasagna sheets
  • 300 g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 2 cups white sauce or ricotta mixture
  • Mozzarella cheese
Instructions

How to Cook It

  1. Cook the beef with onion until browned, then stir in the tomato sauce.
  2. Spread a little sauce in a baking dish.
  3. Layer pasta sheets, meat sauce, white sauce, and cheese.
  4. Repeat the layers until the dish is full and top with cheese.
  5. Bake until bubbling and golden on top.
