Recipe 1

Paella

Paella is a saffron rice dish cooked in a wide pan with chicken, seafood, and vegetables.

Ingredients

What You Need

  • 1 cup paella rice
  • 200 g chicken pieces
  • 200 g shrimp or mussels
  • 1 tomato, grated
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • A pinch of saffron
  • Olive oil, garlic, and peas
Instructions

How to Cook It

  1. Brown the chicken in olive oil, then add garlic and grated tomato.
  2. Stir in the rice so it is lightly coated.
  3. Pour in the saffron stock and spread the rice evenly.
  4. Add shrimp or mussels and peas, then simmer without much stirring.
  5. Cook until the rice is tender and lightly crisp at the bottom.
Recipe 2

Gazpacho

Gazpacho is a cold tomato soup blended with vegetables, olive oil, and vinegar for a refreshing Spanish dish.

Ingredients

What You Need

  • 6 ripe tomatoes
  • 1 cucumber, peeled
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 2 slices bread
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • Salt and cold water if needed
Instructions

How to Make It

  1. Roughly chop the tomatoes, cucumber, and bell pepper.
  2. Blend the vegetables with bread, olive oil, vinegar, and salt.
  3. Add a little cold water if the soup is too thick.
  4. Chill in the refrigerator until very cold.
  5. Serve cold with a drizzle of olive oil.
Recipe 3

Spanish Omelette

Spanish Omelette, or tortilla, is a thick potato and egg dish cooked gently in a pan until soft and golden.

Ingredients

What You Need

  • 4 potatoes, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 6 eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
Instructions

How to Cook It

  1. Cook the potatoes and onion gently in olive oil until soft, not crisp.
  2. Drain off most of the oil and let the mixture cool slightly.
  3. Beat the eggs with salt and mix them with the potatoes.
  4. Pour the mixture into a pan and cook until the base sets.
  5. Flip carefully or finish the other side gently until cooked through.
