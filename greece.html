Recipe 1

Moussaka

Moussaka layers eggplant, meat sauce, and bechamel, then bakes everything until golden.

Ingredients

What You Need

  • 2 eggplants, sliced
  • 300 g ground beef or lamb
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups bechamel sauce
  • Olive oil, salt, and pepper
Instructions

How to Cook It

  1. Salt the eggplant slices, pat them dry, then roast or pan-fry until lightly browned.
  2. Cook onion, garlic, and the ground meat until browned.
  3. Stir in the tomato sauce and simmer until the filling thickens.
  4. Layer eggplant and meat sauce in a baking dish, then pour bechamel on top.
  5. Bake until the top is golden and let it rest before slicing.
Recipe 2

Souvlaki

Souvlaki is marinated skewered meat grilled until lightly charred and served with bread or salad.

Ingredients

What You Need

  • 500 g chicken or pork, cubed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • Salt and pepper
  • Skewers
Instructions

How to Cook It

  1. Mix olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Marinate the meat in the mixture for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Thread the meat onto skewers.
  4. Grill or pan-sear until cooked through and lightly charred.
  5. Serve hot with pita, yogurt sauce, or salad.
Recipe 3

Greek Salad

Greek Salad is a fresh mix of tomatoes, cucumber, onion, olives, and feta dressed with olive oil.

Ingredients

What You Need

  • 3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cup olives
  • 150 g feta cheese
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp oregano
Instructions

How to Make It

  1. Place tomatoes, cucumber, onion, and olives in a large bowl.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle oregano over the vegetables.
  3. Toss gently so the vegetables stay intact.
  4. Add the feta on top in chunks or cubes.
  5. Serve fresh and chilled if preferred.
